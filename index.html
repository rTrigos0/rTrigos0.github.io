Rossett Acre School Pantomime

My first large scale solo show operation.

For this show I was given very little input from the company so I was given free reign over the design of the lighting.
Sound effects and music were run through Cuelab, radio mics were used for primary cast members and singers.
Visuals were simply images so they were run through a company made PowerPoint.
After receiving a copy of the script i annotated it with cues for lighting, sound and visuals allowing me to self cue
the entire show effectively.
